To ensure all your passkeys stay in sync, you need to use the same vault on all your devices. To consolidate everything into one primary vault, you can do so by sharing them one by one:

  1. Open the Passkeys App on the device with the account you want to move.

  2. Select the account, swipe left, and tap Share.

  3. Confirm you understand the security notice.

  4. Send a Share Link to your main device.
