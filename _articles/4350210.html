Your profile’s QR code is used when someone wants to invite you to join a shared group in the Password Manager app. Here’s how to find it:

  1. Open the Password Manager on your device.

  2. Head to the Settings section.

  3. To the right of your name, tap the QR Code icon shown.

Your unique QR code will now be displayed. You can either show this QR code to the person inviting you or share it as a screenshot if they are not nearby.

ℹ️ Tip: Your QR code contains your secure profile connection info. Only share it with people you trust.
