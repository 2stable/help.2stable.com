With the Authenticator App widget, you can view your 2FA codes even faster, right from your Home Screen on iPhone and iPad or Notification Center on Mac. This gives you instant access to your most-used accounts without needing to open the app.

Depending on your device, you can follow the specific guide below:

  • Add the Authenticator App Widget on iPhone or iPad

  • Add the Authenticator App Widget on Mac

Each guide walks you through the steps for setting up the widget and customizing which accounts appear. You can even add multiple widgets to show different 2FA codes.

ℹ️ If you don’t see the widget as an option, make sure the Authenticator App is installed and updated to the latest version on your device.
