To enable iCloud Drive for the Authenticator App, follow these steps:

  1. Click the Apple menu in the top-left corner of your screen and select System Settings.

  2. In the System Settings window, tap on [your name] at the top to access your Apple ID settings.

  3. In the right sidebar, click on iCloud and then tap on the Drive folder.

  4. Look for Sync this Mac and make sure it’s toggled ON.

Once you’ve completed these steps, iCloud Drive will be enabled for the Authenticator App.
