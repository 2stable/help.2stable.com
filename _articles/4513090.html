To choose our Passkeys App by 2Stable as your default passkey app, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Settings app on your device.

  2. Scroll down and tap General.

  3. Tap Autofill & Passwords.

  4. Under Autofill From, make sure Passkeys App is toggled ON.

ℹ️ Note: This feature is available on devices running iOS 17 or later.

Once set, your device will prefer our app when setting up passkeys on your accounts, saving you time and steps.
