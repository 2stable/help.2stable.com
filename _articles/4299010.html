To ensure all your 2FA codes and accounts stay in sync, you need to use the same vault on all your devices. To consolidate everything into one primary vault, you can choose from the following options:

Option 1: Share accounts one by one

  1. Open the Authenticator App on the device with the account you want to move.

  2. Select the account, swipe left (or right-click on Mac), and tap Share.

  3. Choose to:

    • Scan the QR code on another device, or

    • Send a Share Link to your main device.

Option 2: Share all accounts using Import/Export

  1. Go to Settings > Import/Export.

  2. Export your accounts from one vault and Import them into the primary one.
