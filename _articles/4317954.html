To choose our Authenticator App by 2Stable as your default Two-factor Authentication app, follow these steps:

  1. Open the System Settings on your Mac.

  2. Scroll down and tap General.

  3. Tap Autofill & Passwords.

  4. Under Set Up Codes In, select Authenticator App by 2Stable.

Once set, your device will prefer our app when setting up 2FA accounts, saving you time and steps.
