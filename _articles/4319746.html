With the Authenticator App Browser Extension, you can quickly autofill your 2FA codes right in your browser, making secure logins easier than ever.

❗ To use the browser extension, you must have the Authenticator App for Mac version 3.15.0 or later installed.

Steps to pair the extension with the app:

  1. Open the Authenticator App on your Mac and unlock it.

  2. Go to Settings, select Browser Extension, and enable Allow Pairing.

  3. Click Pair New Browser Extension – this opens the scanner window.

  4. On your browser (Safari, Chrome, or Firefox), go to: authenticator.2stable.com/extensions

  5. Scroll to your preferred browser and click Install to add the extension.

  6. Once installed, a QR code will appear in the browser.

  7. Use the scanner window in the Authenticator App to scan the QR code.

After successful pairing, you’ll see a green message: “Ready to AutoFill 2FA Codes.”

ℹ️ Note: The Authenticator App extension icon on your browser (Safari, Chrome, or Firefox) will also update from a grey shield with green checkmark to a blue shield with white checkmark.
